Summary of Methodology We Used for This Project

When we were handed our project assignment in class, we each committed to review the material given to us regarding the project, meet to discuss our understanding, and at that point create a high level plan

Our initial meeting, a kick off so to speak, was on March 14th, at Yaletown Starbucks. We:

  • reviewed our understanding of the project goals
  • discussed the requirements that to clarify our understanding
  • discussed whether we had any clarifications and/or questions we needed to ask Alistair
  • created an initial high level project plan that would take us thru the next week
  • setup a time and logistics for our next discussion
  • We agreed we could meet as required using Webex and Conferencing facilities, thus allowing us to avoid needing to travel to have face to face meetings
  • committed to each completing a review of a minimum of two sites before our next meeting, which we felt could be similar in their purpose to what Alistair felt the COMP 1950 site currently is and should be

On March 22nd, we met for the second time and at this meeting:

  • reviewed the site assessments we had done, and discussed strengths and weaknesses of those findings
  • discussed standards that we would use in completing our project, including coding standards
  • decide on how we would manage the outputs and artifacts
  • created a full project plan and assigned creation of key wireframes and also one key deliverable from that plan to each team member and set a completion milestones for those of March 25th

On March 26th at the next class, we met to review our key deliverables and progress. We then:

  • made any refinements to our project plan
  • assigned all remaining tasks evenly, fairly, amongst our team members
  • set milestones thru to the end of our project assignment submission deadline
  • set and scheduled two additional checkpoint meetings to review progress
  • set an internal goal to have all of our content and basic layout completed for our project site by Monday, March 30th
  • Allowed time to completed any formatting and final adjustments to our project content and site between March 31st and prior to class starting on April 2nd.

Project Introduction

Our team members have been enrolled in a BCIT part time course, COMP1950 The course is titled Web Development and Design 2.

For our final assignment, course attendees were grouped into teams, by our instructor, Alistair Calder. The teams were assigned a project that was to produce a concept describing how the current course website used by Alistair could be redesigned to better suit his need as well as the students.

This website is the result of completing that final project assignment.
